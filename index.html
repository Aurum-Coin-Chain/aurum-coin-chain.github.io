🔷 The Next Step: AURUM Blockchain & Vision

In phase two, AURUM becomes independent. Its own blockchain launches – lightweight, accessible, and fully backed by real Bitcoin. No central provider, but a decentralized system that maintains itself.

Anyone holding AU can join the network using simple hardware. Lightweight nodes secure the system efficiently – similar to Bitcoin miners. Node operators are rewarded with mining coins (with halvings) and transaction fees.

Participation is open – no special equipment needed. AURUM is open source. The community drives development: features, interfaces, improvements – all shaped together. No admin. No control. Code is law.

The BTC reserve remains the anchor – growing transparently and permanently. Every AU is backed by real BTC with a provable floor, but no ceiling.

Redemption of AU for aBTC (AURUM's Bitcoin format) includes a 21% penalty. The redeemed AU is burned – increasing scarcity and protecting the reserve.

The penalty discourages short-term thinking: opportunists lose. Only long-term holders benefit.

AU is a share in that digital vault. A system that secures real Bitcoin – open, censorship-resistant, and lasting.
